About Alabs Fresh

Our story, mission, and values

Our Heritage

Founded in 2015 by Adeola and Abimbola Johnson, Alabs Fresh began as a small family business sourcing authentic African ingredients from local farmers in Nigeria. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a thriving enterprise as demand for high-quality Afro foods expanded globally.

Our Mission

We are committed to bridging the gap between African farms and global kitchens. Our mission is to deliver the authentic taste of Africa while supporting sustainable farming practices and fair trade principles.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: We preserve traditional preparation methods
  • Quality: Only the finest ingredients make it to your table
  • Sustainability: Ethical sourcing and eco-friendly packaging
  • Community: Supporting local farmers and producers
